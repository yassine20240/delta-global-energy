Our Services

Integrated intelligence, advisory, and capital solutions across maritime and global energy markets.

Maritime Intelligence

Comprehensive maritime intelligence services providing real-time and historical AIS vessel tracking, port congestion analysis, vessel movement monitoring, and maritime traffic analytics. Our solutions deliver accurate insights into global shipping activity, supporting operational planning, risk assessment, and informed commercial and strategic decision-making across international maritime markets.

Trade Flow & Commodity Analytics

Advanced trade flow and commodity analytics offering global visibility on bulk, container, and tanker movements. We identify commodity origins, destinations, cargo volumes, trade routes, and market dynamics, enabling clients to assess supply and demand patterns, monitor trade corridors, and anticipate market trends across global energy and commodity sectors.

Shipping & Logistics Advisory

Strategic shipping and logistics advisory services designed to optimize fleet deployment, chartering strategies, port operations, and supply chain efficiency. We support shipowners, charterers, and operators with expert guidance on vessel utilization, route planning, operational performance, and logistics decision-making across global maritime networks.

Maritime Finance & Vessel Brokerage

Specialized maritime finance and vessel brokerage services covering asset finance, leasing structures, sale and purchase advisory, and chartering support. We advise clients on transactions involving newbuildings and second-hand vessels, providing commercial insight, market intelligence, and execution support throughout the lifecycle of maritime assets.

Energy Advisory – Oil & Gas

Comprehensive energy advisory services across the oil and gas sector, including commercial, legal, and transaction advisory. Our expertise covers acquisitions and divestments, due diligence, hydrocarbon sales, upstream strategy, portfolio optimization, and support for complex energy transactions in international markets.

Strategy, Economics & Training

Strategy, economics, and training services delivering advanced economic modeling, strategic consulting, and industry-focused training programs. We support executives, investors, and operational teams with structured analysis, decision frameworks, and knowledge transfer tailored to the energy and maritime sectors.
